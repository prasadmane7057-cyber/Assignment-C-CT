My Favourite Movies

1)3 Idiots


movie link

This movie teaches the importance of following our passion and not just running behind marks and pressure.

Summary of the movie

3 Idiots is about three engineering students who study at a prestigious college.
Rancho believes in learning with understanding instead of just memorizing for exams.
His different thinking changes the lives of his friends Farhan and Raju.

The movie shows:
  • Pressure of education system
  • Importance of true learning
  • Value of friendship and dreams
Important Message of the Movie
  • Follow your passion
  • Don’t run behind success, gain knowledge
  • Friendship is very powerful
  • Think creatively, not just by memorizing

2)Dangal

A motivational movie based on wrestling and the strong bond between a father and his daughters.

Summary of the movie

Dangal is a biographical sports film based on the real life of wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat.
He dreams of winning a gold medal for India but could not achieve it himself.
So, he trains his daughters Geeta and Babita to become world-class wrestlers despite facing many social challenges.
The movie shows their hard work, struggles, discipline, and success in wrestling competitions.

Important Themes / Message
  • Women empowerment
  • Importance of hard work and discipline
  • Never give up on dreams
  • Parents’ dedication for children’s success

3)Bahubali: The Beginning


movie link

An epic historical action movie with amazing visuals and storytelling.

Summary of the movie

Baahubali: The Beginning tells the story of Shivudu, a young man who discovers his true identity as Mahendra Baahubali.
As he learns about his past, he finds out that his father Amarendra Baahubali was a great king of the Mahishmati kingdom.
The movie shows Shivudu’s journey to rescue Queen Devasena and to know the truth about his royal family and kingdom.

Important Themes / Message
  • Courage and bravery
  • Fight between good and evil
  • Power, loyalty, and sacrifice
  • Importance of truth and justice
